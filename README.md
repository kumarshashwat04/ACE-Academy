ACE Academy — Ranger RTP Certification Guide

Welcome to the internal product knowledge certification platform for GreyOrange. This repository/guide outlines the onboarding steps, certification paths, assessment structures, and recommended timelines for getting certified on the **Ranger RTP** platform.

## 🚀 Quick Start
* **App URL:** [ace-academy][https://ace-academy-azure.vercel.app/]
* **Access Requirements:** Accessible via the **GreyOrange internal network** (no VPN required from the office). Contact your team admin for login credentials.

## 📚 Syllabus & Study Focus

Click on **Syllabus** in the left sidebar to access your learning modules. Always study modules in logical order (e.g., starting with *1.1 Platform & Infrastructure*).

* **PathFinder Focus:** Product concepts, flow purposes, and system architecture.
* **Navigator Focus:** UI steps, microservice interactions, and system logic.
* **Grand Master Focus:** Backend diagnostics, commands, database tables, and troubleshooting.

💡 *Each module card includes a **Confluence Doc** link for deep reading and a **Video** walkthrough if available. Remember to click **Mark as Done** to save your progress!*

## 📝 Assessment Details

Assessments are timed and auto-submit when the countdown expires. 

| Level | Format | Time Limit | Pass Mark | Focus Area |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :---: | :--- |
| **PATHFINDER** | 20 Multiple-Choice | 30 mins | **75%** | Product concepts, flow purposes, system architecture |
| **NAVIGATOR** | Multiple-Choice | 60 mins | **75%** | UI flows, microservice interactions, system logic |
| **GRAND MASTER** | Scenario-Based | 90 mins | **80%** | Backend diagnostics, Mnesia, Kafka tracing, PostgreSQL queries |

*Note: If you do not pass, you can review the syllabus and re-attempt **immediately** with no waiting period or attempt limits.*

## 📅 8-Week Training Roadmap (TAC Track)

The full path starts immediately following documentation finalization on **15 June 2026**.

* **Weeks 1–2 (PathFinder):** Platform & Infrastructure (1.1.x), Pick Flows, Put Flows, and Audit Flows. *PathFinder Assessment at the end of Week 2.*
* **Weeks 3–4 (Navigator):** Deep dive into UI steps, microservices, and system logic. *Navigator Assessment at the end of Week 4.*
* **Weeks 5–7 (Grand Master):** Backend commands, DB tables, Kafka tracing, Mnesia diagnostics, and real ticket walkthrough practice.
* **Week 8 (Grand Master Assessment):** Live troubleshooting assessment leading to full certification.

*(If your role only requires PathFinder, follow Weeks 1–2. If it requires up to Navigator, follow Weeks 1–4).*
